Welcome to Weather Forecasting

Weather forecasting involves predicting atmospheric conditions for a specific location and time using data from satellites, radar, weather stations, and computer models.

  • Current Conditions: Real-time data on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and atmospheric pressure.
  • Hourly and Daily Forecasts: Predictions for upcoming hours and days, helping with short-term and long-term planning.
  • Humidity Levels: Information on the amount of moisture in the air, crucial for understanding comfort levels and precipitation chances.
  • Wind Speed and Direction: Data on wind velocity and direction, important for activities like sailing, flying, and outdoor events.
  • Severe Weather Alerts: Notifications for extreme weather events like storms, hurricanes, and tornadoes, ensuring safety and preparedness.
  • Interactive Maps: Visual representations of weather patterns, including radar images, precipitation, and temperature variations.
  • Climate Data: Historical weather data for analyzing trends and patterns over time.
